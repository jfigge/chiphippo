Features

A real bench, without the wire mess

Everything you need to design a 74xx circuit, prove it out, and take it to a real breadboard — fast, keyboard-friendly, and fully offline.

Infinite breadboard desk

An endlessly pannable, zoomable workspace. Drop Full 830, Half 400, and Tiny 170 boards — power rails and pin-boards snap together like the real thing.

50+ 74xx chips

Gates, flip-flops, counters, decoders, muxes, shift registers, comparators, and bus drivers — a data-driven catalog with datasheet-exact pinouts. Add a chip; it's just data.

Live simulation engine

Trace power from the supplies, resolve every electrical net, compute each chip's outputs, and ripple changes until the circuit settles. LEDs glow, switches act live.

Sequential logic & clocking

Edge-triggered flip-flops, counters, and shift registers step correctly on every clock. A clock source drives the bench with Run / Pause / Single-step and speed control.

Memory chips

Address-indexed ROM, SRAM, and EEPROM on wide DIP packages. Drive the address bus and the stored byte appears on the data pins; write it back on a /WE pulse.

Switches, LEDs & power

Slide switches, momentary push buttons, four-colour polarised LEDs, and 3 V / 5 V / 12 V supplies with addressable terminals. Wrong voltage? Watch a chip release its magic smoke.

Net probe & inspector

Hover any hole to highlight its entire electrical net across every board and wire, with a live readout of what's connected and the level it's carrying.

Buses

Wire a whole address or data bus in a single gesture instead of one lead at a time — the bus tool tracks matching pin groups across chips and keeps the routing tidy.

Schematic view

Flip from the physical breadboard to a clean logical schematic — each part drawn as its symbol, nets auto-routed — so you can read the design the way an engineer would.

Build guide & wiring list

Turn a finished design into artifacts you can follow at a real bench: named nets, a step-by-step build guide, and a wiring list to place every chip and route every jumper.

Pinouts & datasheets

Double-click any part for a floating pin-assignment window — the physical DIP diagram plus the manufacturer's connection diagram and function table, right on the desk.

Offline & open source

No account, no telemetry, no cloud. Everything runs locally and your designs are plain files you own. Apache-2.0 licensed and fully auditable on GitHub.
